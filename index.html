About me

Hi there, welcome to my first website!

My name is Christina and I am a passionate Translation Technology Specialist. From the very first moment in the field of translation, I decided to be involved in translation technology. I have noticed that using proper translation memories and termbases can make the translation process more efficient, of higher-quality, and accomplished in less time.

I handle every step of managing translation memories and termbases; from creation and organization to updating, editing and ensuring they work seamlessly within CAT tools. In addition, I bring a keen eye for detail to my work as an Editor & Proofreader, ensuring that content is clear, accurate and consistent.

I'm now pursuing an M.Sc. in language technology.

In my free time, I enjoy working out, practicing martial arts, and traveling.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn if you'd like to learn more!
